How to use

Select a filtering method and Mediterranean region. Click on any CID sector or label in the radial diagram to display anomaly ranges for increasing global warming levels. Hover over the anomaly bars to inspect numerical values.

Filtering methods

Method A: exclusion of simulations rated as Bad.
Method B: inclusion of simulations that reach GWL4 and are not rated as Bad.
Method C: inclusion of all simulations.

Displayed information

The radial plot summarizes confidence in projected CID changes and historical trend evidence. The detail panel shows ensemble mean anomalies, P10–P90 ranges, min–max spread and the number of simulations.
